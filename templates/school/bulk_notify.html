Channel Guide
In-App
Recipients see the notification inside the Kiswate web app when they next log in. Parents view it at Parent Portal → Notifications. Students view it at their notification page. Zero cost, instant delivery.
Email
Sent to the address on the user's account. Requires email to be configured in settings. Good for long messages, documents, and links.
SMS
Delivered to the phone number on record via the EUJIM / AdvantaSMS gateway. Requires SMS credentials in settings. Best for urgent short alerts. Costs apply per message.
